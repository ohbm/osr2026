## Emergent Session Submissions

{::options parse_block_html="true" /}
<p align="justify">
  **Emergent sessions** are live discussions among a small group of panel members, mediated by a host and open to audience of the OSR. The discussions may cover more advanced topics related to open-science or EDI (Equality, Diversity and Inclusion) issues.
</p>
<p align="justify">
During the OSR, attendees can hold **hybrid emergent sessions**. <br> 
The current 2026 OSR program includes **6 slots** for application. All times below are shown for Bordeaux in the `Europe/Paris` timezone, which is CEST (UTC+2) during the June 15-18, 2026 meeting:<br>
</p>

### Review timeline

<p align="justify">
Emergent-session requests are reviewed on a rolling basis during the meeting. We aim to confirm accepted proposals quickly while slots remain available so organizers have time to prepare hybrid participation and promotion.<br>
<br>
If you need a rapid decision on a timely proposal, please submit the form and contact us either by email at `ohbmopenscience@gmail.com` or in person at the OSR.<br>
