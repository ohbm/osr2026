ShortBio: Dr. Anazodo is the William Dawson Scholar and Assistant Professor of Neurology
  and Neurosurgery at the Montreal Neurological Institute, McGill University where her
  group develops PET and MRI techniques for quantitative neuroimaging. Dr Anazodo is very
  passionate about improving access to diagnostic imaging with a focus on population and
  global health. She is the founder and Chair of the Consortium for Advancement of MRI
  Education and Research in Africa (CAMERA), a global network of MRI experts working to
  establish sustainable access to high-value MRI in Africa through local capacity building.
  She also established the Medical Artificial Intelligence (MAI) Lab at Crestview Radiology
  Ltd in Lagos, Nigeria, Africa's first AI lab dedicated to training, development, and
  implementation of AI applications in medical imaging. Through these efforts, her group
  develops and shares open dataset, analysis models, and findings to close gaps in
  Neuroimaging, especially across Africa.
